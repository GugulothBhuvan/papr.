\begin{abstract}
    \raggedright
    This report provides a comprehensive overview of Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG), a prominent paradigm in large language models (LLMs) designed to address limitations such as hallucination and knowledge cutoff. We delve into the core principles, architectural components, training methodologies, and evaluation strategies of RAG. Furthermore, we explore its diverse applications, current challenges, and future research directions, aiming to offer a holistic understanding of this impactful technology.
\end{abstract}
